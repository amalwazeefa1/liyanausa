DG Classes

Dangerous Goods Classes We Handle

We provide specialized handling for all 9 classes of dangerous goods with tailored solutions for each category.

Explosives

Materials that can explode under certain conditions including ammunition, fireworks, and blasting caps.

  • Division 1.1-1.6
  • Mass explosion hazard
  • Special permits required

Gases

Flammable, non-flammable, toxic and compressed gases including propane, oxygen, and chlorine.

  • Flammable gases
  • Non-flammable gases
  • Toxic gases

Flammable Liquids

Liquids with flash points below 60°C including gasoline, alcohol, paints, and solvents.

  • Flash point < 60°C
  • Packing groups I-III
  • Combustion hazard

Flammable Solids

Solids that are easily combustible including matches, sodium batteries, and metal powders.

  • Flammable solids
  • Spontaneously combustible
  • Dangerous when wet

Oxidizing Substances

Materials that can cause or enhance combustion including peroxides and nitrates.

  • Oxidizing substances
  • Organic peroxides
  • Enhanced combustion

Toxic & Infectious

Materials harmful to human health including pesticides, medical waste, and toxic chemicals.

  • Toxic substances
  • Infectious substances
  • Health hazard

Radioactive

Materials emitting ionizing radiation including medical isotopes and nuclear fuel.

  • Radioactive I-III
  • Special packaging
  • Radiation control

Corrosives

Materials that can dissolve organic tissue including acids, alkalis, and batteries.

  • Acids & alkalis
  • Tissue damage
  • Material corrosion

Miscellaneous

Other hazardous materials not covered above including environmentally hazardous substances.

  • Miscellaneous hazards
  • Environmental hazards
  • Elevated temperature
