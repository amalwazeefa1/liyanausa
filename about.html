Our Story

Founded in 2008, Liyana Logistics USA INC began as a small freight forwarding company with a vision to revolutionize the logistics industry. From our humble beginnings in New Hyde Park, NY, we've grown into a global logistics powerhouse serving clients across six continents.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, strategic partnerships, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. We've navigated through industry challenges, adapted to technological advancements, and expanded our service portfolio to meet the evolving needs of global trade.
