Cross Trade Models We Handle

We provide specialized solutions for various cross trade models to meet diverse international business needs.

Direct Cross Trade

Goods shipped directly from supplier to buyer with documentation handled through intermediary.

  • Direct shipment
  • Documentation management
  • Payment processing

Triangular Trade

Goods shipped between three countries with complex documentation and payment flows.

  • Multi-country coordination
  • Complex documentation
  • Multi-currency payments

Back-to-Back LC

Using two separate letters of credit to facilitate trade between supplier and buyer.

  • LC management
  • Banking coordination
  • Document presentation

Transit Trade

Goods passing through intermediate countries with specific transit documentation.

  • Transit documentation
  • Customs bond management
  • Route optimization

Switch Trade

Documentary transfer of goods between countries with changed documentation.

  • Document switching
  • Title transfer
  • Payment redirection

Consignment Trade

Goods shipped to intermediary for eventual sale to final buyer in different country.

  • Inventory management
  • Sales coordination
  • Revenue distribution
